Path of the Torah

[2]

  • The five books of the Torah, from Genesis to the prophets and writings, Mishnah Gemara with Rashi's commentary and other holy exegetes. That is how they always studied and learning the Holy Siddur leads to the Holy Gemara and Shulchan Aruch and Sefer Torah.
  • Every father is obliged to raise his children with tutors and proficient honest, and holy work, faithfully worshiping and observing and fearing the Lord which they need to provide for the young and pure child’s heart. They should plant love for the Lord into them, to the Torah and to the commandments and to pray from childhood on and to recite every blessing, respond with Amen, His name is great, holiness, blessings and the like.
  • The teacher and tutor needs to be an expert and teacher, he must be a devout person. He therefore needs to be a mirror for his students. With a tutor like this, the parents can be assured that their child will become an honest, good, pious Jew and also a scholar like the teacher.
  • The teacher must educate the child in mitzvot in action and practice, observing Sabbath, etc., honouring their parents, respecting the elders, fear of God and King.
  • As it is the duty of every Jew to keep to the established times for prayer and Torah reading, is it the duty of every father to visit the classroom often, to take care of the Siddur, question the child himself or ask each other to question the child. Every father and mother needs to know that their classroom is educating their children to honest Jews and this cannot happen without the Torah. The Holy Torah is eternal and does not change with time or fashion. As we say: I believe with complete faith that the Torah will not pass away, and there will be no other Torah from the Creator, blessed be His name. And with the Torah, the Jewish people will survive all ages and fashion.
